Fresh Garden Salad

Description

This fresh garden salad is crisp, colorful, and full of nutrients. A combination of lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and other vegetables makes it perfect as a side dish or a light meal. You can customize it with your favorite dressing or add toppings like nuts, cheese, or avocado for extra flavor and texture. Quick to prepare, this salad is a healthy and refreshing choice any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed lettuce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • Your favorite salad dressing

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop or slice vegetables as desired.
  3. Combine all vegetables in a large salad bowl.
  4. Add olives or other optional toppings if desired.
  5. Drizzle with your favorite dressing and toss gently.
